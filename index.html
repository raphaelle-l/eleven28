ABOUT

The Firm

Eleven28 Architecture + Design creates thoughtful, site-responsive residential architecture rooted in context, craft, and stewardship. We approach each project with clarity around design, budget, and construction, delivering work that is enduring, responsible, and deeply connected to place

Eleven28 Architecture is a Boulder-based architectural studio providing residential design services throughout Colorado's Front Range. Founded by licensed architect Renee Golobic, the firm is known for thoughtful, site-responsive design rooted in technical rigor, regulatory fluency, and a deep understanding of how people live in—and move through—their homes.


Eleven28 Architecture is closely involved in every phase of each project, from early feasibility studies and entitlement analysis through design development, permitting, and construction coordination. The firm works collaboratively with clients, builders, engineers, and city staff to ensure that design intent is carried through from concept to completion. Rather than pursuing a singular stylistic approach, Eleven28 prioritizes balance—aligning client goals with site constraints, neighborhood context, budget realities, and what is realistically approvable for each property.


The practice has extensive experience with remodels, additions, accessory dwelling units, and new residential construction, including projects within sensitive regulatory environments. Eleven28 regularly works with historic and older homes and has deep familiarity with the City of Boulder's Historic Preservation Program and related review processes. This experience allows the firm to navigate complex approvals efficiently while advocating for architecture that respects existing character and supports contemporary living.


At its core, Eleven28 Architecture is committed to clarity, durability, and stewardship—creating well-crafted spaces that respond intelligently to place, policy, and the long-term needs of the families who inhabit them.

Renee Golobic, AIA

Founder + Principal Architect

Renee Golobic is the Founder and Principal Architect of Eleven28 Architecture, a Boulder-based residential practice serving communities across Colorado's Front Range. With over a decade of experience, she brings a disciplined, site-specific approach to residential design, guiding projects from early feasibility through permitting and construction.


Her work is informed by a deep understanding of local codes, historic contexts, and entitlement processes, allowing her to translate complex constraints into clear, livable architecture. Renee is a licensed Architect in Colorado and currently serves as Chair of the Boulder Landmarks Board, contributing a civic-minded perspective to her practice.
